Delicious Homemade Chili

Chili is a good, hearty meal that is perfect for colder temperatures. It's also incredibly easy to make. Let's take a look at how to whip some up.

Ingredients

  • One Pound Ground Beef

    A medium blend beef works, but you can opt for even ground turkey if you'd prefer.

  • One Onion

    The size is entirely up to your preferences. I aim for a medium sized onion.

  • One (15oz) Can of Tomato Sauce

  • One (15 oz) Can of Kidney Beans

  • One and 1/2 Cups of Water, or As Needed. (Not Required)
