Delicious Homemade Chili

Chili is a good, hearty meal that is perfect for colder temperatures. It's also incredibly easy to make. Let's take a look at how to whip some up.

Ingredients

  • One Pound Ground Beef

    A medium blend beef works, but you can opt for even ground turkey if you'd prefer.

  • One Onion (diced)

    The size is entirely up to your preferences. I aim for a medium sized onion.

  • One (15oz) Can of Tomato Sauce

  • One (15 oz) Can of Kidney Beans

  • One and 1/2 Cups of Water, or As Needed. (Not Required)

  • One Teaspoon Chili Powder (or To Taste)

  • One Pinch of Garlic Powder

  • Salt and Pepper to Taste

Steps

Throwing it together is simple and doesn't require much prepwork.

  1. Add ground beef and onion to a large saucepan over medium heat. Cook and stir meat until it is browned and the onions are tender. Usually 5 - 7 minutes.


  2. Stir in tomato sauce, kidney beans, and stewed tomatoes with juice. If you prefer a thinner consistency, you can add water. Season with chili powder, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper.
    Bring to a boil, reduce heat to low, cover and let simmer for 15 minutes.
